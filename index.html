Rules of the Game

  • 1. Bumping into an opposing player that is on your side will "tag" them and teleport them back to their side of the field, where they'll remain frozen for a few seconds.
  • 2. Bumping into the flag will pick it up and attach it to the player.
  • 3. Bringing it back to their side will score a point for the team.
  • 4. Once a certain point value is reached, the game is won by the team reaching that value.
