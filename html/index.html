Rules of the Game

  • 1. Game starts when there are 2 players on each side.
  • 2. Bumping into an opposing player that is on your side will "tag" them and teleport them back to their side of the field, where they'll remain until they heal.
  • 3. Bumping into the flag will pick it up and attach it to the player.
  • 4. Bringing it back to their side will score a point for the team.
  • 5. Once time runs out, the game is won by the team with the highest score.
