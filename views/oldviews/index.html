Rules of the Game

  • 1. Bumping into an opposing player that is on your side will "tag" them and they will have to walk back to their side of the field to be untagged at base.
  • 2. Bumping into the flag will pick it up and attach it to the player.
  • 3. Bringing it back to their side will score a point for the team.
  • 4. The game ends after 2 minutes and the team with the higher score wins!
